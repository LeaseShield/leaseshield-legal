Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2026  ·  Last updated: 1 July 2026

LeaseShield ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is stored and used, and your rights in relation to that data. By using the LeaseShield mobile application ("the App"), you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

LeaseShield is a mobile application designed to help Dubai-based tenants understand their rights, manage their lease obligations, and communicate professionally with landlords. The App is operated by Logan Riley, trading as LeaseShield, and is available on iOS and Android.

Contact: leaseshield.app@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and authentication method (Google Sign-In or email/password), collected when you create an account.
  • Lease information: Details you enter about your tenancy, including landlord name, property address, lease start/end dates, annual rent, and cheque schedule. This data is stored solely to power the App's features and is never shared with third parties.
  • Document scans: If you use the AI document scanning feature, images or PDFs you upload are processed by our AI systems for data extraction and then discarded. We do not retain uploaded documents.
  • Usage data: Anonymised analytics on how you interact with the App (screens visited, features used, session duration) to help us improve the product. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Crash and error data: Anonymous crash logs and error reports to help us identify and fix technical issues.
  • Subscription data: Your subscription status (Free or Pro) is managed via RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details — all payment processing is handled directly by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  • Device and notification tokens: FCM (Firebase Cloud Messaging) tokens are stored to deliver push notifications such as cheque payment reminders. You can opt out of notifications at any time via your device settings.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the App's core features (lease management, AI analysis, email generation, cheque reminders)
  • To send you push notifications you have opted into (e.g. cheque due date reminders, lease expiry alerts)
  • To verify your subscription entitlement and unlock Pro features
  • To anonymously analyse usage patterns and improve the App
  • To diagnose and resolve technical issues via crash reporting

We do not use your data for advertising purposes. We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore database and Firebase Storage), with our primary server region set to me-central1 (Doha, Qatar) — the closest available Google Cloud region to the UAE. Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Data at rest is encrypted by Firebase using AES-256.

Access to your data in Firebase is controlled by Firestore security rules that ensure you can only read and write your own records. No other user can access your lease or account data.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, storage, push notifications, and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-powered lease analysis and email generation, processed server-side. Your lease content is sent to Anthropic's API for processing and is subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not store this content after processing.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management and entitlement verification. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — payment processing for subscriptions. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

6. UAE Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)

LeaseShield is committed to compliance with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL). Under this law, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at leaseshield.app@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You can also delete your account and all associated data directly from within the App via Account → Delete Account. This action permanently removes all your data from our systems.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data (including lease records, cheque schedules, and account information) is permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated analytics data is retained indefinitely as it cannot be used to identify you.

8. Children's Privacy

LeaseShield is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will notify you via the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
