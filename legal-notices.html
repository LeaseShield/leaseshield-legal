1. Disclaimer — Not Legal Advice

LeaseShield provides informational tools and AI-generated guidance to help tenants understand their rights and manage their tenancy in Dubai. All content, outputs, and recommendations within the App are for informational and educational purposes only. Nothing in this App constitutes legal advice, and no lawyer-client relationship is created by your use of the App.

Specifically:

  • AI Lease Screener: Clause analysis results are automated assessments based on general knowledge of UAE tenancy law and RERA regulations. They are not a legal review and may not account for your specific circumstances, recent regulatory changes, or the interpretation of a qualified lawyer.
  • AI Email Generator: Email templates are drafted to be professional and legally informed, but they are not reviewed by a lawyer and should not be treated as formal legal correspondence in proceedings.
  • RERA Rent Calculator: Outputs are estimates based on the RERA Rent Index and publicly available data. Actual permitted rent increases may differ. Always verify with RERA directly or a qualified property advisor.
  • Termination Calculator: Notice period and penalty estimates are based on general interpretations of UAE Law No. 26 of 2007 and its amendments. Individual lease terms and circumstances may affect the outcome.
  • Renewal Timeline: Dates and deadlines provided are informational reminders only. You are responsible for verifying your specific lease obligations.

For any dispute, legal proceeding, or situation where professional judgment is required, we strongly recommend consulting a qualified UAE lawyer or contacting RERA directly.

2. RERA and UAE Law References

LeaseShield references the following UAE legislation and regulatory frameworks:

  • UAE Law No. 26 of 2007 regulating the relationship between landlords and tenants in the Emirate of Dubai, as amended by Law No. 33 of 2008
  • RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) Rent Index and Rent Increase Calculator
  • UAE Federal Law No. 6 of 2019 on Ownership and Use of Real Property
  • UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL)

Laws and regulations are subject to change. LeaseShield makes reasonable efforts to keep content current but cannot guarantee that all references reflect the most recent legislative amendments. Always verify current regulations with RERA or a legal professional.

  • Should be reviewed critically before acting on it
  • Does not reflect the views or opinions of LeaseShield or Anthropic
  • Is not a substitute for professional legal, financial, or regulatory advice
